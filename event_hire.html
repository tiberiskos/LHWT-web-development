Event Hire

Make your event one to remember with our beautiful, one-of-a-kind location.

Having recently gained permission to use our Windmill site for a wide range of events, we are delighted to offer it as a venue for birthday parties, bridge drives, film nights, charity dinners or whatever else you might be planning.

As you would expect of such a historic site, we limit the number of events we hold to just six per year, so book early to avoid missing out.

We also rent out the windmill as a location site for photo and video shoots, as well as TV and film work. For more info call Peter James on 07881 951400.

Useful info

Availability; the site is available to hire from May to September every year. If you would like to check a specific date please see contact below.

Local accommodation; due to our proximity to Gatwick Airport, there are many local B&Bs and hotels to suit a wide range of budgets.

Accessibility; Capacity is up to 80 people with parking. we do not allow access to the top part of the Mill, which means the site is fully wheelchair accessible. Please note that in wet weather, you may require matting on the grass to aid wheelchair access.
